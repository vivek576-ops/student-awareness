🫁 4-7-8 Breathing Exercise

Calm your nervous system and reduce stress instantly.

  • 👃 Inhale through nose — 4 seconds
  • 🫁 Hold your breath — 7 seconds
  • 💨 Exhale through mouth — 8 seconds
  • 🔄 Repeat 4 times

💡 Do this every morning and before exams!
