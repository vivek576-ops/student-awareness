🫁 4-7-8 Breathing Exercise

This technique helps calm your nervous system and reduce stress instantly.

  • 👃 Inhale through nose for 4 seconds
  • 🫁 Hold your breath for 7 seconds
  • 💨 Exhale through mouth for 8 seconds
  • 🔄 Repeat this cycle 4 times

💡 Do this every morning and before exams to stay calm!

💆 Stress Management Tips

  • 📚 Break study sessions into 25-minute blocks
  • 🚶 Take a 10-minute walk between study sessions
  • 💧 Drink plenty of water throughout the day
  • 😴 Get at least 7-8 hours of sleep every night
  • 📵 Avoid social media 1 hour before sleeping
  • ✍️ Write down worries to clear your mind
  • 🎵 Listen to calming music while studying

💡 Small consistent habits beat last-minute cramming!
